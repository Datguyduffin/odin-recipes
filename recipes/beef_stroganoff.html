Beef Stroganoff

Description

A super-easy stroganoff with little-to-no fuss. It smells wonderful and is tasty,
like no other stroganoff you've tasted before. Remmber, server over hot,
buttered flat noodles, but I am sure any noodles will do good with this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds cubed beef stew meat
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1(10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon dried chives
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1(8 ounce) container sour cream
  • 1(8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 ounces of cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Slow cooker
  • Mixing bowl

Steps

  1. Spread beef stew meat into bottom of slow cooker crock pot;
    season withsalt and pepper. Layer onion over the beef.
    Pour muschroom soup and water over the beef;
    then add chives, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, and beef bouillon.
  2. Whisk red wine, cornstarch, and flour together in a small mixing bowl;
    pour the mixture in to the slow cooker.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 to 7 hours. Stir sour cream, mushrooms, cream cheese, and parsley
    into the beef mixture; continue cooking for 1 more hour.
