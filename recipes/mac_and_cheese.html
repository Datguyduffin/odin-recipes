Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • Saucepan
  • Large casserole dish
  • Skillet

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni according to the package directions, up to draining, then stop.
  2. Using a saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat.
  3. Stir in enough flour to make a roux.
  4. Add milk to roux slowly, stirring constantly.
  5. Stir in cheeses, and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and the sauce is a little thick
  6. Put cooked macaroni in large casserole dish, and pour the sauce over the macaroni, stir well.
  7. Melt butter or maragine in a skiller over medium heat
  8. Add breadcrumbs to the skillet, then spread over the macaroni and cheese in the large caseroll dish
  9. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 minutes. Serve
